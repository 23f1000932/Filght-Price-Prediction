Exploratory Data Analysis

Key insights derived from the dataset visualization.

Airline Frequency

Insight: Jet Airways and Indigo dominate the dataset frequency. Budget carriers have higher volume but lower average prices.

Price Distribution by Airline

Observation: Jet Airways Business is a massive outlier with prices roughly 4x the average. Standard Jet Airways is also premium priced compared to Indigo/SpiceJet.

Impact of Total Stops

There is a direct positive correlation. As stops increase, price increases due to fuel and airport charges. 1-stop flights are the most common trade-off.

Route Economics

  • Most Expensive Source Delhi
  • Cheapest Source Chennai
  • Avg Duration 10h 30m

Feature Importance

After training the Random Forest Regressor, we analyzed which features contributed most to the predictive power of the model.

Key Takeaway

"Total Stops" is the single most dominant predictor of flight price, followed by the specific day of the journey and the overall duration.
