Deciphering
Flight Pricing Logic

An advanced regression analysis aimed at predicting flight ticket prices with high precision. Leveraging machine learning to understand how variables like days left, airline class, and departure time drive costs.

The Challenge

Participated in the MLP Term 2 2025 Competition. The goal was to build a model that minimizes prediction error (RMSE) on unseen test flight data.

My Approach

Focused on Feature Engineering. Created 'Days_Left' from date columns, categorized 'Departure_Time', and handled mixed data types in the 'Flight_Code' column to extract valuable signals.

The Solution

Deployed a LightGBM Regressor. It outperformed Random Forest in training speed and handled the high cardinality of 'Source' and 'Destination' categorical features efficiently.
